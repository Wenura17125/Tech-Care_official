# Error Report: Missing Technician Background Images in PC Repair Services

## Project Name
TechCare – PC & Laptop Repair Platform

## Module
PC Services → Technicians Section

## Error Title
Technician Background Images Not Displayed

## Environment
- Platform: Web Application
- Browser: Google Chrome (Desktop)
- Operating System: Windows 11
- Hosting: Netlify
- Page: PC Repair Service Page

## Description
In the PC Repair Services page, technician cards are displayed without their background images. The image area appears empty or with a default background instead of the expected technician images.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the TechCare website.
2. Navigate to **Services → PC Repair**.
3. Scroll to the technicians section.
4. Observe the technician cards.

## Expected Result
- Each technician card should display a background image.
- Images should load correctly for all technicians.

## Actual Result
- Background images are not displayed.
- Technician cards appear without images.

## Impact
- Poor visual presentation of technicians.
- Reduces user trust and attractiveness of the service page.
- Makes technician profiles less informative.
